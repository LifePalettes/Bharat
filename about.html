About

I believe that imagery is powerful

and that honouring

our bodies,

emotions,

choices,

impulses

through photography

is sublimely empowering,

and profoundly encapsulaing.

I’m a self-declared dreamer

and have been infatuated with visual storytelling

since I picked up my first camera,

depicting emotions..

I have a penchant for capturing emotional escapades,

black and white bodyscapes,

and making simple moments

unforgettable.

I’m on a mission

to empower people, through my art.
